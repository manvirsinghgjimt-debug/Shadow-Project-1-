Coding Club Activities at GJIMT

The Coding Club at GJIMT is an active student-run organization that organizes weekly coding challenges, hackathons, project showcases, and peer mentoring sessions. It serves as an informal extension of the academic curriculum where students can practice beyond regular lab hours and explore problems of their own interest.

Club members regularly participate in external competitive programming platforms such as LeetCode, HackerRank, and CodeChef. Seniors mentor juniors in preparing for placement-oriented problem solving, focusing on time complexity, edge case handling, and clean code practices. The club also conducts internal contests with small prizes to keep motivation high.

Special sessions are held before placement season to simulate interview conditions. Students solve problems under time pressure, present solutions to peers, and receive feedback. The Coding Club is one of the most popular extracurricular activities among MCA students at GJIMT due to its direct impact on career readiness.
