AI Lab at GJIMT – Tools, Projects, and Learning

The AI Lab at GJIMT is a dedicated space where MCA students apply artificial intelligence tools in real academic and project contexts. The lab is equipped with systems that have access to ChatGPT, Gemini, GitHub Copilot, Grammarly AI, and Notion AI — all tools that students use under guided and documented conditions.

Lab sessions are structured around weekly activities aligned with shadow projects. Students do not simply generate code using AI. Instead, they learn to prompt effectively, evaluate AI suggestions critically, and document which parts of their work were AI-assisted versus independently written.

Projects completed in the AI Lab range from Mini Google Search Engine implementations in C to data analysis tasks in Python. Faculty supervise lab work to ensure that students understand every line of code they submit. The lab environment trains future developers to treat AI as a powerful collaborator rather than a shortcut.
