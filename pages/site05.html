Placement Opportunities for MCA Graduates at GJIMT

MCA graduates from GJIMT are well positioned for careers in software development, data analysis, systems design, AI engineering, and IT consulting. The AI-integrated curriculum ensures that students graduate with both fundamental programming knowledge and hands-on experience with modern AI-assisted development workflows that employers value highly.

The placement cell at GJIMT maintains relationships with IT companies across Chandigarh, Mohali, Noida, Bengaluru, and Hyderabad. Campus drives are conducted regularly throughout the final year, and students receive guidance on resume writing, technical interview preparation, and communication skills through dedicated workshops.

Students who perform well in shadow projects, maintain strong DSA skills, and actively participate in the Coding Club consistently receive placement offers in competitive packages. The AI lab experience also gives students a conversational edge in interviews where companies ask about practical exposure to AI tools in software development workflows.
