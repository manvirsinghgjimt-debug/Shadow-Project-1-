AI-Integrated MCA Program at GJIMT

GJIMT offers a forward-thinking MCA program that integrates artificial intelligence tools directly into the curriculum. Students learn programming, data structures, algorithms, and software engineering alongside practical AI tool usage including ChatGPT, Gemini, GitHub Copilot, Grammarly AI, and Notion AI.

The program is designed to produce graduates who can build real-world software systems while applying AI-assisted development methods responsibly. Each semester includes shadow projects benchmarked against industry leaders like Google, preparing students for competitive placements in top technology companies.

Admissions are open to science graduates with mathematics at 10+2 or graduation level. The program emphasizes hands-on lab work, peer collaboration, and structured documentation. Students are assessed not only on code output but also on their ability to explain logic, compare approaches, and reflect on AI tool usage.
